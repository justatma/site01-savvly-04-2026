# Savvly Brand Colors

## Primary
- Brilliant Blue: #3478EB (primary accent, links, CTA buttons, headline accent words)
- Vivid Blue: #2B3D9A (dark blue, navigation bar, deep backgrounds)

## Secondary
- Baby Blue: #A7C7E7 (light accent, hover states, subtle backgrounds)
- Pollon Yellow: #ECD06F (warm accent, use very sparingly, never metallic gold)

## Neutrals
- White: #FFFFFF (primary background)
- Near-black: #1A1A2E (body text, dark sections)

## Rules
- No default Tailwind blues. Use only these values and their derivatives.
- No cream, gold, teal, or warm palettes. Those signal insurance/legacy fintech.
- Pollon Yellow appears only as a small accent, never as a dominant color.
- The dominant visual impression should be white space + Brilliant Blue.
